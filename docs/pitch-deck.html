02 / 09
The Solution

Primus — EHR reimagined
for primary care

  • Single-Screen Workflow

    Providers complete 90% of tasks without navigating away from the patient chart. Everything — vitals, meds, notes, orders — in one place.

  • Keyboard-First Design with Command Palette

    Every common action reachable in < 3 clicks or keystrokes. Press Ctrl+K for instant search across patients, orders, and actions.

  • HIPAA-Compliant, Cloud-Native SaaS

    Multi-tenant architecture with row-level security, full audit logging, encrypted at rest and in transit. AWS-hosted with 99.9% uptime SLA.

  • Built for Every Role — From Provider to Patient

    8 distinct roles, each with tailored dashboards and workflows. One platform for the entire clinic operation.

10-phase delivery roadmap

Phase 1

Real patient records, appointments, calendar API, check-in workflows.
Phase 3

Phase 4
Orders + Labs
Lab ordering, Quest HL7 integration, results inbox, critical value routing.
Phase 5
e-Prescribing
ScriptSure EPCS integration, PDMP, controlled substance workflows.
Phase 6
Billing + RCM
Claims, Availity clearinghouse, ERA processing, Stripe patient payments.
Phase 7
Telehealth
Amazon Chime SDK integration, waiting room, in-chart video consults.
Phase 8
Notifications
Twilio SMS, email reminders, in-app notifications, patient communication.
Phase 9 + 10
Analytics + SaaS
Reports, HEDIS dashboards, multi-tenancy RLS hardening, HIPAA audit, production.

Each phase: build → test → sign off → next phase. No phase starts until the previous is approved.
